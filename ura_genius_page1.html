The Zero-Cost Model
How Dual Pricing Works

Dual Pricing (also called Cash Discounting) is a legal, transparent model that lets you pass card processing fees to customers who choose to pay by card — while rewarding cash-paying customers with a small discount.

💳

Card Customers Cover the Fee

A small surcharge on card transactions funds your processing costs — completely transparently disclosed.

💵

Cash Customers Get a Discount

Customers who pay cash receive the "cash price" — creating a win for them and zero processing costs for you.

🎁

Your Equipment is 100% Funded

The savings from Dual Pricing cover the full cost of your Genius hardware, software, and installation.
